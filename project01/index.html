Project 1: Getting to Know You

Description

Here's Gabriel's Getting To Know You project from EN 1 (Fall 2022). If you get the chance, please use the documentation station in Nolop to take good pictures (unlike this example)!

Some general tips for documentation:

  • Take a picture with a plain background and nothing distracting in the shot. The Nolop documentation station makes this easy!
  • Lighting is key! Use lights from multiple angles and try to avoid shadows, especially from yourself.
  • Take photos horizontally.
  • Don't forget to include a video to show your robot in action!
